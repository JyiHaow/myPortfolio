Hi there, I'm Liew Jyi Haow, a 25-year-old male currently residing in Petaling Jaya, Selangor looking out for opportunities to not only expand my skillset but also broaden my knowledge in both art and technology.

I am a graduate of Computer Science (Interactive Multimedia) from Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka (UTeM). After graduation, I went to Silver Ant Sdn. Bhd. for my internship and landed a job as a Character Effects Artist in Silver Ant PPI Sdn. Bhd. During my time in the company, I got to work on several Japanese projects such as "Ronja the Robber's Daughter", a japanese anime series.

Programming

Multimedia Production
